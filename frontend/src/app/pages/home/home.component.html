Home Energy Monitor

Track, Analyze, and Optimize Your Energy Usage

The Home Energy Monitor app helps users track their energy consumption, manually input data, upload CSV files, and receive alerts when their energy usage exceeds predefined thresholds. The goal is to provide users with insights into their energy consumption and encourage smarter energy usage.

Core Features

Secure Authentication

Protected access using AWS Cognito authentication services

Data Management

Manual data entry and bulk CSV uploads for historical data

Smart Alerts

Real-time notifications via AWS SNS when usage exceeds thresholds
